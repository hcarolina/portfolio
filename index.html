About

Hello, my name is Carolina Hernandez and I am a student studying Mechanical Engineering at Indiana University - Purdue University Indianapolis. I am also learning HTML, CSS, and Python through ElevenFifty Academy. You might be thinking why is a ME student learning any sorts of programming. When I had to take a C programming class for my major, I was really intrigued with programming and the process behind it. When I learned that there was a winter program being taught, I signed up as soon as I could.

The reason for wanting to futher my knowledge in code was for my own personal experience, but also for my career. I feel it doesn't hurt to know more about another line of work, learning more can only help you, never hurt you. As for what I will do with this certificate in programming, I'm not sure. But I do know what I will make use of the new knowledge that I gained during the Winter coding experience.
